Chocolate Cookie - The Easy Way

Do you feel like having some chocolate cookies with with milk?

Please, follow these simple steps to become the master of Christimas!

Ingredients:

Toffe:

  • 1 Stick Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tsp kosher salt

Brown Butter:

  • 2 Sticks Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Ice Cubes

Dough:

  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Cups Dark Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tsp Espresso Powder
  • 2 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Sode
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tsp Good Vanilla Extract
  • 2 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 6 Oz Chocolate, 70% Cacao Minimum
  • Maldon Sea Salt

Steps:

  1. Melt the unsultade butter
  2. Mix all ingredients for the Toffe till it's uniform
  3. Stir fry the the 2 sticks of unsalted butter with the ice cubes
  4. Mix all the ingridients for the dough till they're uniform
  5. Put the dough on the fridge for two hours
  6. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
