Japanese Potato Salad - The Easy Way

I know, it seems like a normal potato salad from the above image.

..but this one is actually different, it's the japanese way of making this delicious meal!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Small Potatoes
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 3-4 Slices Ham
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3-6 Tbsp Mayonnaise (your preference)
  • 1 Tsp Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Cut and boil potatoes and eggs at the same time
  2. In the meantime, slice cucumbers, cutting out the seeds if you need to
  3. Put cucumbers into a plastic bag along with 1/4 tsp salt.
  4. Give it a good massage and set it to the side to let excess water come out
  5. Slice ham and set aside
  6. Take out eggs after 12-15 minutes and put into cold water
  7. Potatoes are done when a stick can easily be pierced
  8. Take off the skins if you don't like them
  9. Slice boiled eggs
  10. Add in the eggs
  11. Squeeze out excess water from the cucumbers and add to the potatoes
  12. Add ham and mayonnaise
  13. Season with salt and pepper to taste
